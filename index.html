The Principal

Dr. Mohamed

Founder & CEO — Astrafay

Physician. Diplomat. McKinsey leader. Public intellectual. Dr. Elfayoumy has spent over fifteen years operating at the apex of government, international institutions, and private enterprise — from the UN and Egypt's diplomatic corps to the Saudi Royal Court and the boardrooms of sovereign capital.

A former McKinsey leader and principal contributor to Saudi Vision 2030, he is also a columnist in Al-Ahram and ThePundit.org, and the host of ElRoshetta — a distinctive podcast on healthcare systems, policy, and transformation.
