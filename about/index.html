Dr. Mohamed
Elfayoumy

Founder & CEO — Astrafay

Dr. Elfayoumy is one of the region's most distinctive strategic voices: a medical doctor by training, a diplomat by experience, a McKinsey leader by practice, and a public intellectual by conviction. His career spans the highest levels of government, international institutions, and private enterprise — giving him a depth of access and contextual understanding that is genuinely rare.

He has served Egypt's diplomatic corps at the most sensitive posting in the Arab world, advised the UN Special Envoy for Syria, led major transformation programmes at McKinsey, and contributed directly to Saudi Vision 2030 — working with the Royal Court, Council of Ministers, and five line ministries.

Today, as Founder and CEO of Astrafay, he advises governments, sovereign institutions, and private enterprises on strategy, transformation, and investment across the GCC, Egypt, and sub-Saharan Africa — while continuing to write and speak publicly on the ideas that matter most.

He is a Yale alumnus, Stanford CDDRL Fellow, and an active investor in healthcare and technology ventures across MENA and the United States.

About Astrafay

Built for work that actually matters.
