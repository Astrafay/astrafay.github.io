The Firm

Built for work
that actually lands.

Astrafay was founded on a simple conviction: that the most consequential advisory work happens when deep regional knowledge meets world-class analytical rigour. We advise governments, sovereign funds, and leading enterprises on strategy, transformation, and investment — across the GCC, MENA, and sub-Saharan Africa.

Effective advisory in this region requires more than technical competence. It requires understanding how decisions are actually made — the political dynamics, the cultural context, and the relationships that determine whether a strategy lands or fails. Our team has operated inside governments and institutions, not just alongside them. That distinction matters.

We work with a select number of clients at any one time. Every engagement is led by a senior practitioner, supported by a team assembled specifically for the mandate. No generalists. No bait-and-switch.

The Principal

Dr. Mohamed
Elfayoumy

Founder & CEO — Astrafay

Dr. Elfayoumy is one of the region's most distinctive strategic voices — a medical doctor by training, a diplomat by experience, a McKinsey leader by practice, and a public intellectual by conviction. His career spans the highest levels of government, international institutions, and private enterprise.

He has served Egypt's diplomatic corps at the most sensitive posting in the Arab world, advised the UN Special Envoy for Syria, led major transformation programmes at McKinsey, and contributed directly to Saudi Vision 2030. He is a Yale alumnus and Stanford CDDRL Fellow, and an active investor in healthcare and technology ventures across MENA and the United States.
